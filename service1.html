General Medicine

The General Medicine department serves as the cornerstone of healthcare delivery, offering a broad spectrum of services aimed at diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide range of medical conditions. Within this department, patients receive comprehensive care from highly skilled physicians known as internists or general practitioners. These professionals are trained to address various health concerns across different organ systems and age groups. Services provided typically include routine health check-ups, preventive care such as vaccinations and screenings for diseases like hypertension, diabetes, and cancer, as well as management of chronic conditions like asthma, arthritis, and heart disease. General Medicine departments also play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating acute illnesses such as infections, respiratory ailments, gastrointestinal disorders, and injuries. Furthermore, they often coordinate care for patients with complex medical needs, collaborating with specialists when necessary to ensure holistic and personalized treatment plans.
